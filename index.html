Meet Amanda and Dan:

Amanda and Dan met and started their journey together in an apartment in Washington, DC. Their first garden started in their apartment six years ago with using vegetable scraps to have scallions available for their cooking. Now they live on a 1/2 acre homestead 5 miles outside the city limits where they are growing, presevering, and preparing a lot of their food. They also have plans to retire at 45.
